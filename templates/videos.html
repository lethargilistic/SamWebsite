Videos and demo reel

Damsel

I made the Damsel for an assignment to make 'a brief commercial' for a fictional company. I am not one to go halfway on anything, so I took great pains to make sure each image looked great, got a good friend (Henrique Neves) to voice act, and wrote up a brief rhyme to make an entire film trailer. This was made in the first quarter of my Digital Design degree, and set the stage for my Digital Design abilities to shine.

Showcase

Done just to examine my abilities in After Effects, this school assignment included putting a bunch of images we've done into a slideshow. Naturally, these are all old drawings of mine, some dating back several years.
