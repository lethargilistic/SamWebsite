Welcome to the Portfolio of Sam Morstan

Samantha Morstan is a writer and artist, working on any number of artistic and literary projects at any given time. Her current projects are tentatively titled Theomachy and Kingdom Cards, a book and a card game, respectively. Samantha has received Two AAs from Pierce College (one general, and one in Digital Design), and a BA in English (creative writing focus) from the University of Washington in Seattle. She has written one play (Doctor, Dealer, Drugged), which was performed by the University of Washington's Undergraduate Theater Society in 2013, as a part of the New Works Festival. She had one picture (Cubist Living Statue) in the 2015 edition of the Student Literary Arts Magazine at Pierce College.

Samantha is always looking for the next creative project, and ready to throw herself into it. Her greatest ambition is to create things that delight and inspire others.
